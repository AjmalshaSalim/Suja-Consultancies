How We Started

Ageis Recruitment and HR Consultancy is a start-up platform for the talented people to step towards excellence and are targeting the emerging company market. Ageis is a Kerala based Human Resource sourcing and staffing company set up in the year 2022. with a vision to exceed the expectations of our customers and clients to develop and manage value added human resources policies and provide expert consultation, services and solutions in efficient and customer focused manner, to provide the necessary tools to meet our customer’s needs.

Ageis will be serving all over India with the aim of giving better staffing and delivering the best candidate, Ageis also seeks to be a talent acquisition firms and recruitment brand in the country. Human Resource Solution is our goal and will provide comprehensive HR solutions through the single window across various domains to enhance the growth of the organization.

Ageis Recruitment and HR Consultancy is commitment to the fair selection and development of our diverse workforce. As a response to the need for employees we believe in building brighter futures and by turning ideas into action to spark positive change.

The first step is to modify the process to be 100% remote. That’s how we are starting to work with remote recruiters, and Talent Place crowd staffing. It is the most effective recruitment method in the market. We strive to hire the right resource for the right purpose.

Our Clients rely on us for their complete staffing requirements ranging from a single “hard-To-Fill” top management positions to massive hiring for junior level positions. We keep the requirements mentioned by the clients on priority to hire the deserving candidates from the entire lot. Whether it is an MNC, a startup or a medium-sized organization, we tailor-made packages catering to all your business requirements. Thus, all of these are services come with cost effective pricing, making clients and job seekers their trusted customers. All projects that we undertake get best of the service without compromising quality but at affordable prices.

Vision

Our vision is to gain success and emerge as a preferred partner for corporate and business houses in the Consulting, Talent Acquisition, and HR Practice space, become the leading consultancy firm by providing quality HR solutions and add value to our clients and customers. We aim to penetrate deep into the recruitment market and our expertise lies in our ability to source highly qualified professionals across a broad range of function and skill sets.

Mission

Our mission is to provide human resource consulting for emerging companies and is committed to the fair selection and development of our diverse workforce, to pave a path for change in the labor market towards quality, modernity and flexibility. For this purpose, we implement business models to support partnership building, innovative and effective methods for sourcing candidates and create the workplace of the future using our own example
